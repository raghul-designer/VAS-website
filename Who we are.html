ABOUT CHAIRMAN

The Chairman of the company Mr. Elangovan.S hailing from the farming family as his father K. Srinivasan is a peasant and having a piece of Land of 4.77 acres dryfield in Kenipallam Surampatty village thumbalam, Thathaiyyengarpet in Trichy district. Due to starvation and draught his father could not perform farming activities and switched to Namakkal and did automobile mechanical activities for his family and kids sustainment.

The Elangovan.S who pursued his studies, schooling from Govt. schools and studied his Agriculture, under graduation at college of Agriculture, Dhule that has been affliated with Mahathma Phule agricultural University, Rahuri, AhmedNagar District, Maharashtra state and well versed in Marathi and Hindi, along with his mother tongue Telugu, Tamil and English language. However has a vision to redress the woes of the farming community across India and aspired for civil service, environment could not got through. To empower became a member of the BJP in the year of 2010 March and working on various positions as District general secretary kissan morcha, for three years, further worked as state executive council member of kissan morcha, amidst Bharatiya kissan sangh as District general secretary, Bharatiya mazdoor sangh for certain months to establish BMS labour gang one of first kind in Railways goods transportation in Tamil Nadu, currently as special invitee cum planning incharge of kissan morcha of BJP the Elangovan S is rendering services to the people.
